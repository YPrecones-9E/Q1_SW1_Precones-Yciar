LAUFEY - Reviving Jazz, One Step at a Time

Laufey (pronounced LAY-vay )is an Icelandic-Chinese singer and musician.
Inspired by legends like Ella Fitzgerald and Chet Baker, she has made jazz- and classical-inspired music more accessible despite their declining popularity.

Why I Love Laufey

  • Heavenly Voice: She boasts an impressive range and precise yet passionate vocals whether in the studio or live.
  • Touching Lyrics: Her songs about love, heartbreak, and nostalgia are reminiscent of Disney movie music with their ability to transport you to different worlds and evoke strong emotions.
  • Multitalented: Alongside songwriting, Laufey can play the cello, piano, and guitar.
